TUNES FOR EVERYONE

Queue strives to enhance the music listening experience when in groups. Queue will be a platform for crowdsourcing music at any event or venue from eating clubs and bars to gyms or coffee shops to small friend gatherings.

Scan

Scan a a QR code at the event to join the Queue! This lets you request and like songs while ensuring only people in attendance can edit the queue.

Queue for Businesses

Use the power of the Queue to increase patron engagement and create an environment that represents your customer base. Sign up for Queue and never have to worry about background music again.

Queue Autopilot allows your business to take song requests directly from patrons. Queue will filter based on genre and a custom mood-determining algorithm to ensure the music fits the theme of your business. Queue also has the ability to disallow explicit songs if desired.

Queue for Individuals

Forget about having to unlock you phone constantly so your friend can add more Kanye or no-looking your password while driving to appease the Swifties.

Queue lets your friends add songs to your Spotify or Apple Music queue from their own devices. Not sure what to add? Queue can reccommend songs based on your liked songs and mood. The Queue app also lets you request songs anywhere Queue is supported.

Frequently Asked Questions

Queue is scheduled for a beta release in third quarter of 2022. We anticipate a full release in early 2023. Check back occasionally for updates.

Queue will be free for all individuals! Due to the inherent cost of servers and maintenance Queue for Business will unfortunately come at a minimal cost.

Queue makes requests through Spotify's secure API which is public and available for commercial use.
